What is a sequence?

  • A sequence is an ordered list of elements or objects, known as terms, where repetition is possible, and the order does matter. A sequence can be made up of anything, but typically it is a list of numbers or other mathematical objects. An example of a sequence is: 1, 1, 2, 3, 5, 8, 13,..., where each element is the sum of the two previous elements, and the sequence goes on infinitely. This sequence is known as the Fibonacci sequence.
